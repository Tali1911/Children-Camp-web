## 1. PROJECT OVERVIEW

### 1.1 Project Summary
Development of the Amuse Ke Platform - a comprehensive enterprise management system for children's outdoor education organizations in East Africa. The platform will transform traditional camp operations through digital automation, integrated departmental workflows, and scalable architecture.

### 1.2 Project Objectives
- **Primary Goal:** Create a unified digital ecosystem for outdoor education management
- **Business Impact:** Reduce operational costs by 65% compared to existing enterprise solutions
- **Market Position:** Establish market leadership in East African outdoor education technology
- **Revenue Model:** Multi-tiered SaaS platform with implementation and support services

### 1.3 Technology Stack
- **Frontend:** React 18, TypeScript, Tailwind CSS, Vite
- **Backend:** Supabase (PostgreSQL, Auth, Storage, Edge Functions)
- **Architecture:** Progressive Web App (PWA) with offline capabilities
- **Integration:** RESTful APIs, Real-time subscriptions, Mobile-responsive design
